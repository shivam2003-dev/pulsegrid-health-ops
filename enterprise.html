For India, by India

Enterprise health operations for districts, states and NHM missions.

ArogyaGrid is designed as a decision-support layer over Indian public-health rails: PHC/CHC workflows, NHM district administration, Ayushman Bharat facility identity, e-Aushadhi/DVDMS stock movement and HMIS reporting.

India-stack integrations

Do not replace existing systems

  • ABDM/HFR-style facility identity for centre mapping.
  • e-Aushadhi/DVDMS adapter for stock, issues, indents and batches.
  • HMIS aggregate reconciliation for official reporting.
  • eSanjeevani service signals for continuity checks.
  • IPHS 2022 norms for facility readiness and score context.
