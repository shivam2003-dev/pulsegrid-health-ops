What makes it hard to copy

01

Voice-first, not app-first

Missed call and IVR are the primary path. SMS is fallback. WhatsApp photo/voice is optional.

02

Risk portfolio, not one crop

The farmer gets safe, balanced, and high-return crop choices tied to water and rupee risk.

03

Sentinel-1 under clouds

SAR wetness evidence keeps working during monsoon cloud cover when optical NDVI is blocked.

04

RSK validation loop

AI triages routine issues. Experts handle the uncertain 10% and create district early warnings.
